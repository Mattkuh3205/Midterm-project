What I like to do

This section will discuss my broad range of interests, split into 3 main categories
Through these 3 major areas, there are many sub interests of mine that stemmed from these

Main page

Aviation

Aviation is one of my newer main hobbies, but my interest in it started when I was around 8 or 9, when my dad took me to my first airshow.

As I got older, I got more into aviation thanks to watching Top Gun. From then I started learning more about planes in general.

Aviation is one of my favorite hobbies because watching planes perform something that should not be done by man is incredible.

Through aviation, I have gotten into flight simulators (MSFS, DCS), Photography for planes (Photography page), and plane spotting

Aviation has also given me an interest in scale model building and history of military vehicles

Music

Music is an incredibly important part of my life. I enjoy creating music and listening to it. My first instrument was guitar, and I have been playing for nearly 10 years now. I own a total of 5 stringed instruments (3 guitars, 1 bass, 1 ukulele), a Moog synthesizer, and a bunch of other smaller instruments

I love music because it is an incredible form of self expression, and without it, life would not be interesting

Music has brought me a lot of different interests, luthiery, vintage instruments/gear, going to concerts, learning music theory, etc...

I have gotten to see bands such as Primus, Earth Wind and Fire, and solo acts like Carlos Santana, Joe Satriani, and Steve Vai
